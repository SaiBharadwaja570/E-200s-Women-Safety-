SAFETY TIPS

Stay Aware of Your Surroundings
Personal Safety
Always be aware of your surroundings, especially in unfamiliar areas. Stay alert and avoid distractions such as using your phone while walking.
Trust Your Instincts
Personal Safety
If something doesn't feel right, trust your instincts and take action to remove yourself from the situation. Your safety is more important than being polite.
Keep Emergency Contacts
Emergency Preparedness
Keep a list of emergency contacts in your phone and ensure they are easily accessible. This includes family, friends, and local emergency services.
